Info/Bio

At only 26 years of age, DJ Kennedy has already established himself as one of the most successful Australian Deejayz within the last four years

Australian DJ and fashion model has proven to be an avid writer of articles and novels. Not so much a fun of sunglasses, but his dreadlocks tied together at the back and his great expertise in moving the crowd with hits completely distinguishes him as the best Deejay In Melbourne city: John Kennedy. The Kenyan native is actually more the outgoing type, very friendly to people and has great sence of humour. The Unmistakably Kennedy, ailing from a local Apondo Kojwach village in Homaby Makes his way to an internationally acclaimed mega star in the Australian entertainment industry. This, I repeat, at only 26 years of age.
